For those who are interested in finding new vegetarian dishes, this is the resource for you. We are a collective of vegetarians, some who have been so since birth, who spend most of our time thinking about food, reviewing restraunts, and creating new recipes.

This is where we share our reviews, our recipes, and our feedback for advancing the vegetarian culinary arts.



Our goals include:

  • aCreating better restraunt menu options for vegetarians
  • Building a downloadable collection of the best vegetarian recipes
  • Providing a resource for finding excellent vegetarian dining options
  • Helping non-vegetarians to see that there are delicious and healthy diet options that have less environmental impact than meat based diets
